## System Overview

### Objectives

The main goal of the app is to make it easier to manage green spaces. It aims to simplify the planning, 
building and upkeep processes to make city life better. Through a platform the app 
aims to allocate resources coordinate teams effectively and boost operational efficiency overall.
The functionality of the application relies on the interplay of various components and modules. 
Key dependencies include data management, task allocation, resource optimization, and reporting 
mechanisms. Each module is intricately connected, allowing for smooth data flow and efficient 
execution of tasks. The system's design ensures robustness and reliability, even in complex 
operational scenarios.

### Main Features

**Skill Registration:**
- As a Human Resource Manager (HRM), I want to register skills that a collaborator may have. 
  - This feature allows HR managers to input and store various skills that collaborators possess, facilitating efficient skill management within the organization.

**Job Registration:**
- As an HRM, I want to register a job that a collaborator needs to have.
    - HR managers can define and record specific job roles that collaborators are required to fulfill, ensuring clarity and consistency in job assignments.

**Collaborator Registration:**
- As an HRM, I want to register a collaborator with a job and fundamental characteristics.
    - This feature enables HR managers to create profiles for collaborators, including their assigned job roles and other essential details.

**Skill Assignment:**
- As an HRM, I want to assign one or more skills to a collaborator.
  - HRM can assign relevant skills to collaborators based on their job roles and proficiency levels, ensuring appropriate task allocation. 

**Automatic Team Proposal Generation:**
- As an HRM, I want to generate a team proposal automatically.
  - This feature automates the process of generating team proposals, streamlining team formation and task assignment based on predefined criteria.

**Vehicle Registration:**

- As a Vehicle Fleet Manager (VFM), I wish to register a vehicle including Brand, Model, Type, Tare, Gross Weight, Current Km, Register Date, Acquisition Date, Maintenance/Check-up Frequency (in Kms).
  - VFM can input and manage vehicle details such as brand, model, type, weight, and maintenance schedules, ensuring comprehensive vehicle tracking and management.

**Vehicle Check-up Registration:**

- As a VFM, I wish to register a vehicle’s check-up.
  - VFM can record vehicle check-up details, including inspection dates, maintenance activities performed, and any issues identified during the check-up process.

**Vehicle Check-up Listing:**
- As a VFM, I want to list the vehicles needing the check-up.
  - This feature provides VFM with a list of vehicles due for check-up, allowing for efficient scheduling and allocation of maintenance tasks.
